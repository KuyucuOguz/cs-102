Project Title: HealMeal
Project description : HealMeal is a desktop application designed for people who want to trace their activity,
calorie consumption, and stay healthy. HealMeal will suggest daily menus considering
their diseases as well. They will be able to see their log history and track their calories. Additionally,
the app will allow the user to have a cheat day according to their progress
through their goal (if they want to lose weight etc.).
